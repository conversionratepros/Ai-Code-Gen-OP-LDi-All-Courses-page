What makes studying at the LDi different

At the LDi, we’re making academic excellence accessible to professional dentists across the globe. With a faculty of the world’s leading dental minds, plus a unique approach to online learning that prioritises hands-on experience, we’re doing online learning differently.

Flexible study

Learn on your terms and in your time, wherever you are. Balance life and work with flexible study.

Virtual Learning Environment

Experience cutting-edge online learning on an intuitive and user-friendly platform, accessible from any computer or mobile device.

Live events

Connect with the LDi community as well as faculty experts and mentors in real-time, just like you would at a university.

Clinical Simulation Kit

Start accepting new patients and apply theoretical knowledge hands-on with our Clinical Simulation Kit and 1:1 case support.

Academic excellence is accessible wherever

Innovative and academically rigorous, online dental courses at the LDi stand out for providing world-class learning in an online environment. Our courses offer full UK Level 7 Accreditation and are highly flexible around your daily routine.

With the use of our revolutionary Virtual Learning Environment, the LDi app, and a Clinical Simulations Kit, shipped to any address across the world when you enrol, students have access to exceptional dental education at their fingertips.

Log in to the VLE from home, in your practice, or even on the go — the VLE is designed to work just like a virtual university, where learning is accessible 24/7 and from anywhere.

Frequently asked questions

What makes LDi's postgraduate dental courses stand out for dental professionals?

The LDi offers UK-accredited PG Dip. programmes, providing rigorous academic foundations and practical training. Our faculty’s guidance, innovative online tools, and a supportive community of professionals set us apart.

How does LDi's Virtual Learning Environment (VLE) enhance the learning experience?

The VLE provides cutting-edge online learning, fostering a dynamic and interactive educational atmosphere. It offers flexibility, enabling you to balance work and study, ensuring an immersive and convenient learning experience.

Can I expect practical training with LDi's dental courses, and how is it facilitated?

Yes, our courses include practical training facilitated through the innovative Clinical Simulation Kit. This hands-on approach ensures immediate application of theoretical knowledge, enhancing your clinical skills.

How does the LDi cater to diverse career aspirations within dentistry?

LDi’s diverse dental courses and diploma programmes cater to various dental interests, from Orthodontics to Restorative Dentistry. Browse our range, each designed for dentists, ensuring an excellent pathway to clinical practice.

What academic accreditation do LDi dental courses hold, and how flexible are they?

LDi courses are accredited with UK Level 7 Accreditation from Eduqual, reflecting academic excellence. Our flexibility shines through e-Learning tools, accommodating your daily routine while delivering world-class dental education.
